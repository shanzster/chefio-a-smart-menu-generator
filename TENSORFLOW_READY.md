## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "Models taking too long to load"
**Cause:** Large model files (~5MB each)

**Solution:**
- Wait 10-20 seconds on first load
- Models are cached after first load
- Subsequent loads are instant

### "Low accuracy"
**Cause:** TensorFlow.js has lower accuracy than cloud APIs

**Solution:**
- Improve lighting
- Center food in frame
- Try different angles
- Use common foods
- Or upgrade to Google Vision later

### "No food items recognized"
**Cause:** Food not in training data

**Solution:**
- Try more common foods
- Improve image quality
- Use manual input
- Or upgrade to Google Vision
